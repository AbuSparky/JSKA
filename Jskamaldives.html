About JSKA Maldives

About JSKA Maldives Japan Shotokan Karate Association is a Shotokan style Association. It is a non political and non profitable association. It is affiliated to World Karate Federation by https://jska.jp/. The Association has a good experience of participating in different National, International Championships, Seminars and Master Training Camps and has made significant contribution in the field of Karate at National and International Level. SKA was formed under the guidance of Kyoshi Pakkir Raffani lead by Sensei Mohamed Thajudeen both masters are registered in Japan Instructors, a former National Champion having experience of more than two decades in different facets of Karate. He started his training of Shotokan Karate in early Eighties under Sensei Gopala krishnan P.K , who first introduced Shotokan, Karate in India. JSKAI is the fastest growing karate organization in India with more than 130,000 students practicing regularly. spacespacespaceThe Association is going forward with the view of Shotokan Karate and is propagating Karate as a method of exercise, sport, a technique of self defense and also as a Japanese martial art. The mindset of members of the Association is very optimistic to promote and develop Shotokan Karate in Maldives and the members are dedicated to their mission. spacespacespaceThe Association currently consists of 5 Karate Training centers with a main dojo i.e. headquarter. The Association is expanding with new schools, clubs, branch dojo, all over Maldives, setting new standards in our ancient fighting art.
